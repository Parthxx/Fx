Echoes of the Ultras

Explore the passionate world of football fan groups, their legendary cultures, and unforgettable moments.

Boixos Nois

Founded: 1980s

Legendary Figures: Joan Laporta, various fan leaders.

Core Values: Catalan pride, independence, and loyalty to FC Barcelona.

Famous Chants: "Visca el Barça!" "Culé for Life!"

Impact on Football Culture: Known for massive tifos, political chants, and strong presence in **El Clásico** matches.

Notable Rivalries: Real Madrid's Ultras Sur, with fierce encounters in **El Clásico**.

Ultras Sur

Founded: 1980

Legendary Figures: Santiago Bernabeu, club historians.

Core Values: Unwavering support, pride in history, and global unity.

Famous Chants: "Hala Madrid!" "Vamos Madrid!"

Impact on Football Culture: Known for intimidating presence at the Santiago Bernabéu with pyrotechnics and vocal chants.

Notable Rivalries: FC Barcelona (Boixos Nois), with politically charged matchups in **El Clásico**.

The Hunters

Founded: 1980s

Legendary Figures: Various working-class leaders, influential matchday supporters.

Core Values: Aggression, loyalty, and passion for Chelsea FC.

Famous Chants: "Keep the Blue Flag Flying High" and "Carefree, wherever we may be."

Impact on Football Culture: Known for their **violent rivalries** and strong presence at away games.

Notable Rivalries: Millwall, West Ham United, and Arsenal.

Newell's Old Boys

Founded: 1903

Legendary Figures: Local fan heroes and club legends.

Core Values: Loyalty, community, and pride in local roots.

Famous Chants: "Vamos Leproso!"

Impact on Football Culture: Known for their vibrant tifos, passionate support, and intense rivalry with Rosario Central.

Notable Rivalries: Rosario Central - "El Clásico Rosarino".

The Red Army

Founded: 1980s

Legendary Figures: George Best, Eric Cantona, and Ryan Giggs.

Core Values: Tradition, pride, and support for Manchester United.

Famous Chants: "Glory Glory Man United!" and "We are Man United!"

Impact on Football Culture: Instrumental in creating the legendary atmosphere at Old Trafford.

Notable Rivalries: Manchester City, Liverpool, and Arsenal.

NSD

Founded: 1990s

Legendary Figures: Jürgen Klopp (manager), fan culture leaders.

Core Values: Unity, loyalty, and passion for Borussia Dortmund.

Famous Chants: "Echte Liebe" and "You'll Never Walk Alone".

Impact on Football Culture: Famous for their **Yellow Wall**, an unmatched display of fan unity and energy.

Notable Rivalries: Schalke 04 - "Revierderby".
